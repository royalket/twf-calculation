OUTBOUND TOURISM WATER FOOTPRINT & NET BALANCE
============================================================

Methodology
-----------
Outbound_m3 = tourists_to_country × avg_stay_days
              × (local_WF_m3_yr / 365) × 1.5

Tourist multiplier 1.5: tourists consume 50% more than local
residents per day (Hadjikakou et al. 2015; Lee et al. 2021).

Local WF: Hoekstra & Mekonnen (2012) national per-capita WF.
Destination shares: India Tourism Statistics 2022, MoT.
