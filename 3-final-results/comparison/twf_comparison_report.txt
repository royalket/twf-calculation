4. KEY FINDINGS
────────────────────────────────────────
• Total TWF decreased 13.5% from 2015 to 2022 (nominal demand growth).
• Agriculture dominates indirect water (>65% of indirect TWF origin).
• Hotel water intensity fell from 1,251.0 to 818.0 L/room/night (-34.6%).
• Indirect water averages 89% of total TWF; direct averages 11% across study years.
• COVID-19 depressed 2022 direct water vs 2019 (lower occupancy, fewer flights).
